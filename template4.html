About KMC-SHARP

KMC-SHARP (Smart Hub for Analytics, Resilience & Planning) is a flagship digital initiative of the Kolkata Municipal Corporation aimed at strengthening urban governance through data-driven planning and technology-enabled decision making.

The platform integrates civic data, analytics, and monitoring tools to support efficient service delivery, infrastructure planning, transparency, and sustainable urban development across Kolkata.
