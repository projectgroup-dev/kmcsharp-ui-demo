Key Initiatives & Priority Programmes

  • Urban Analytics & Decision Support

    Data-driven monitoring and evaluation system to support evidence-based urban governance under KMC-SHARP.

  • Climate Resilience & Risk Planning

    Integrated planning framework addressing flood risk, heat stress, and climate vulnerability in Kolkata.

  • GIS-based City Infrastructure Mapping

    Comprehensive spatial mapping of civic assets to improve service delivery and planning accuracy.

  • Capacity Building & Training

    Training programmes for municipal officials in data analytics, GIS tools, and smart planning practices.
