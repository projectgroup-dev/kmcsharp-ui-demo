OUR CIVIC BODY

We're Setting the Standards in Urban Governance & Public Services

The Kolkata Municipal Corporation (KMC) SHARP is the governing body responsible for civic infrastructure and administration in the city of Kolkata, West Bengal. Established in 1876, KMC SHARP strives to deliver efficient and inclusive urban services like water supply, sanitation, solid waste management, road maintenance, and health services to over 4.5 million citizens. With over a century of civic experience, KMC SHARP continues to modernize infrastructure and embrace digital transformation to serve Kolkata better.
